At Booz Allen Hamilton (Sep 2015 - Mar 2019), I helped architect this JAX-RS/Spring Boot microservice for the VA, generating formatted PDF documents from requests. Wrote detailed docs on its tech stack. It's much larger than it seems but I was able to reduce the code from about 20 modules of business logic down to 2 microservices and latest Java 17 etc. It was a big achievement and I received multiple company awards at Booz Allen for my efforts on it including a Team and Performance award and Innovation in Action award.
