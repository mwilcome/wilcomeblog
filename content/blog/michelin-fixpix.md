From Aug 2022 to Dec 2023 at Orange Bees, I worked on the Michelin FixPix backend using Java, Apache Camel, Vaadin, Velocity, and Spring. A challenging yet rewarding project enhancing tire service workflows. This supported their On Call systems where technicians doing live roadside service needed quick communication support in rural areas getting locations and many other data points that took technical skill to understand and lead efforts on.
